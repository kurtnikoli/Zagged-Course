Module 01

Content
Guidelines

Common creator mistakes and how to fix them. Follow these rules to make content that actually performs.

The 5 Rules

Master these and you're already ahead of most creators.

1
🔄

Not Following
the Brief

What's Wrong

Creating content from scratch instead of recreating viral formats as requested. Original content is NOT what's needed right now.

How to Fix It

Check the brand brief and see what the right CTA is for this content.

2
📖

Weak
Storytelling

What's Wrong

The product appears out of nowhere with no connection to the previous slides. It feels like an ad interruption, not a natural recommendation.

How to Fix It

Build toward the product: Setup → Problem → Solution (product). Every slide should lead the viewer one step closer to it.

3
👤

Profile
Congruence

What's Wrong

The profile icon shows one person but the videos feature someone completely different. Viewers notice and it kills credibility instantly.

How to Fix It

Profile photo and video content must show the same person. If you changed your look, update the icon. They must match.

4
🖼️

Low Quality
Images

What's Wrong

Blurry, pixelated, or obvious Google stock photos. They signal low effort and kill trust before the viewer even reads the text.

How to Fix It

Use only high-resolution, original-feeling photos. If it looks like a stock image, it is. Replace it. No exceptions.

5
🎵

Wrong Music

What's Wrong

The background music clashes with the content's tone. A mismatch makes viewers feel something's off, even if they can't name it.

How to Fix It

Match the energy: calm content = soft music, hype content = upbeat track. Music shapes 50% of how people feel watching.
